About Sapling & Flint

Sapling & Flint is a jewellery manufacturer specializing in gold & sterling silver. We are a CCAB Certified Aboriginal Business, owned by Mohawk Nation citizens Dakota & Jesse Brant and operating fully on Six Nations of the Grand River Territory, supporting our local Indigenous economy.
